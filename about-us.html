Twin Rivers

Twin Rivers Resort Tigoni, lies along the banks of the adjoining Ruaka and Tigoni rivers hence the name Twin Rivers Resort. The remarkable beautiful carpet-like tea plantations, incredible views, and its cold misty mornings make twin Rivers Resort a private piece of paradise away from the masses!
Whether you are an individual, couple, family, group of friends for organised trip; Twin rivers Resort Tigoni has a package for you.
Looking for a place to spend a weekend, a full day out or a farm stay for a duration, we will create the memorable experience.

You will have a chance to engage in fun activities including Camping, Farm tours, Picnicking, Nature strolls and jogging through undulatinfg tea plantations as well as a wide range of Games, Cycling trips, Darts or just chilling at the farm.

It is ideal for other outdoor activities like weddings, team building, tea and wine tasting sessions, Yoga and meditation events, corporate advertising events, Chamas, Birthday hangouts, Photography and videography

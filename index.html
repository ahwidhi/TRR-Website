About Us

Discover..

..the hidden gem of Tigoni..

Welcome to the enchanting Twin Rivers Resort Tigoni. An idyllic haven where we master the art of transforming fleeting moments into enduring memories.

Situated along the picturesque Kentmere- Redhill loop off Limuru road in Kenya, our resort unfolds like a captivating story,revealing a harmonious fusion of natural splendor and an array of diverse activities designed to create an experience that lingers in your heart.
